Mom's Christmas Cookies

Description

Every time someone asked for her cookie recipe, she said, "Over my dead body!"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup oleo
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 tso. vanilla
  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 tsp.baking powder
  • 1 tsp.salt

Steps

  1. Add alternately with 1 cup cream
  2. Chill and roll out with flour
  3. Bake 350 degrees oven, and frost
